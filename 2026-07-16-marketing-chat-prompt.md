## What DistroFi is

A **paycheck-based budgeting PWA** at https://distrofi.org/app (landing page: https://distrofi.org). The core idea: you don't budget by calendar month — you budget by **pay period**. Each paycheck, you plan: income in, then bills, subscriptions, savings, investments, 401(k), debt payments, and category spending come out, leaving a live "Remaining" balance. When the period ends, it archives to history and the next one starts, anchored to your actual payday (weekly, biweekly, semi-monthly, or monthly).

**Feature set (current, July 2026):**
- Pay-period planning with live Remaining balance, over-budget warnings, and a What-If calculator
- Bills & subscriptions: due dates, priorities, swipe-to-pay, partner "who paid?" tracking, push reminders (3 days before + day of), annual subscriptions amortized monthly
- Spending buckets with budgets, transaction log with search, **per-bucket rollover** (Pro — unused budget carries to next period, envelope-style)
- Savings goals with a "waterfall" (goals fill top-to-bottom), target dates, on-track/behind status
- Investment tracking + goals, 401(k) contribution/match tracking, net-worth view
- Debt payoff planner: avalanche vs. snowball, extra-payment and lump-sum simulators, credit utilization nudges, interest accrual
- **AI Financial Advisor** (Pro) grounded in the user's actual numbers, plus proactive spending-anomaly insights
- **Couples mode**: real-time partner sync with a privacy option (share bills & spending only — income stays on-device)
- Budget health grade (A–F), pay-period history, spending trends, financial lessons library, community polls, CSV/PDF/JSON export, 10 currencies, light/dark themes
- "Your pay period has ended" push notification to drive the next planning session

**Pricing:** Free forever (unlimited core tracking — genuinely, no quantity caps). Pro Solo **$2.99/mo**, Couples **$4.99/mo**. 7-day free trial (14 days via referral link). Referral program: friend gets 14 days free, referrer gets a free month when the friend subscribes.

**Also in the app:** a Members hub with a merch shop (Printful — tees, hoodie, cap, stickers, member discount codes), curated partner deals (SoFi, Webull, Coinbase, Upside — affiliate), and digital products (budget templates/guides).

## Positioning & differentiators
1. **Budgets the way people are actually paid.** Monthly budgets break for biweekly earners; DistroFi's whole model is the paycheck cycle.
2. **Private by design.** No bank linking, no Plaid, no transaction scraping — manual entry means your bank credentials never leave your bank. Couples can share bills without sharing income. No ads.
3. **Radically cheaper than YNAB** (~$15/mo) with a real free tier, unlike most "free" budget apps that paywall the basics.
4. **It's a PWA** — installs from the browser, works on anything, no app-store cut (also means no app-store discovery; that's a marketing challenge to solve).

## What DistroFi is NOT (don't claim these)
- No bank account syncing/aggregation (deliberate, but don't pretend it's coming soon)
- Not in the Apple App Store / Google Play — PWA install only
- No stock market watchlist/live prices (removed July 2026)
- The AI advisor is educational, not licensed financial advice — always keep that disclaimer in copy
