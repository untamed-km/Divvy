Questions people actually ask

Why doesn't DistroFi link to my bank?

On purpose. Manual entry means we never ask for your bank credentials — no Plaid, no screen-scraping. It also keeps you aware of every dollar, which is half of what makes budgeting work.

Is it really free?

Yes. Core tracking is free forever with no quantity caps — not a stripped-down trial. Pro adds rollover and the AI advisor; Couples adds partner sync.

I'm paid semi-monthly / weekly. Does that work?

Yes — weekly, biweekly, semi-monthly, and monthly. Periods anchor to your actual payday.

What happens when my pay period ends?

It archives to history, you decide what to do with any leftover money (save it, roll it, or leave it), and the next period starts automatically.

Is the AI advisor financial advice?

No — it's educational, grounded in your own numbers. It is not licensed financial advice.
